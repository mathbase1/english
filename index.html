What this build now enforces

Will not reward

  • long sentences or academic tone on their own
  • generic openings like “The writer uses language to show…”
  • coverage without analysis
  • basic quotation use being treated as “judicious”
  • clear explanation being mistaken for perceptive analysis

Will penalise

  • copied extract or near-copied extract
  • feature spotting and misused terminology
  • generic reader-effect phrases like “creates an effect”
  • hedging, contradiction, and unsupported symbolism
  • literal comments that do not move beyond the surface

Level 4 only if

  • analysis is consistently accurate and grounded
  • there are multiple genuinely interpretive sentences
  • evidence is selective rather than merely relevant
  • the response sustains high-quality AO2, not just clear explanation
