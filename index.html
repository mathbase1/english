What this build now enforces

Will not reward

  • long sentences or academic tone on their own
  • quote listing without explanation
  • generic phrases like “creates an effect”
  • method spotting without accurate effect
  • copied extract or near-copied extract

Will penalise

  • unsupported symbolism / invented abstract claims
  • misused terminology
  • uncertainty and contradiction
  • generic reader-effect waffle
  • responses that are blank, incomplete, or not answers

Level 4 only if

  • analysis is consistently accurate and grounded
  • evidence is selective rather than dumped
  • effects are specific, not vague
  • there is genuine interpretation, not just technique labels
